Outline for planned Entity Component System 

Base Classes: 
    Tile: 
        Walls and floors 
    Entity: 
        Player, enemies, items 

Components: 
    combat_component: 
        fields: 
            base_hp 
            current_hp 
            base_mana 
            current_mana 
            defense 
        methods: 
            attack() 
    item_component: 
        fields: 
            use_function 
        methods: 
            use() 
    heal_component: 
        fields: 
            heal_amount 
            heal_type 
    spawn_component: 
        fields: 
            min_level - Earliest level parent can spawn 
            max_level - Latest level parent can spawn 
            chance - Chance parent can spawn 

Health potion example: 
entity 
--item_component() 
----heal_component(heal_amount=10, heal_type='hp') 
--spawn_component(min_level=1, max_level=10, chance=70)
