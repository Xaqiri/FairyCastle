Outline for planned Entity Component System 

CombatSystem: 
    damage(attacker, target) 
        Attacker deals damage to target 
    statsComponent: 
        hp, mp 
PhysicsSystem: 
    positionComponent: 
        position 

    draw() 
        Draws the entity to the screen 
    open(obj) 
        Change obj's sprite to its 'open' sprite 
    close(obj) 
        Change obj's sprite to its 'closed' sprite
